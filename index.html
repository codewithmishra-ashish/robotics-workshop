AUTOMAX Robotics Workshop

Join our 2-day immersive workshop at MANIT Bhopal to master Arduino, sensors, CAD, and build real robotics projects. Unleash your innovation!

Register Now

Workshop Details

Day 1: Foundations

Morning: Introduction to Microcontrollers & Arduino Programming (Hands-on: LED Blink Project)
Afternoon: Sensor Integration (Ultrasonic, IR Sensors) & Basic Circuits
Instructor: Prof. V. Agrawal, Mechanical Engineering, MANIT

Day 1: Advanced

Evening: Hardware Interfacing & Motor Control (Hands-on: Line-Following Robot)
Explore real-world applications in automotive robotics.

Day 2: Design & Build

Morning: CAD Basics with Fusion 360 (Design a Robot Chassis)
Afternoon: Capstone Project: Build & Program an Obstacle-Avoiding Robot
Instructor: Dr. S. Gupta, Robotics Club Mentor

Day 2: Showcase

Evening: Project Presentations & Competition
Certificates awarded to top performers.

Prerequisites: Basic programming knowledge (C/C++ recommended). No prior robotics experience required.
Tools Provided: Arduino Kits, Sensors, Laptops, CAD Software.
Venue: MANIT Bhopal, Computer Centre (G9, NKN Lab).

Robotics Culture at MANIT

Technosearch Legacy

MANIT’s annual fest, Technosearch, features robotics events like ‘Mud Warrior Saga’ and hovercraft competitions, drawing 1000+ students. Workshops on automobile robotics inspire innovation.

Robotics Club NITB

The Robotics Club NITB leads initiatives like the E-Yantra Robotics Competition (IIT Bombay) and organizes workshops on embedded systems and IoT.

National Competitions

MANIT students compete in national automotive engineering events, with projects like robocars showcased at Technosearch, fostering a competitive spirit.

Incubation Centre

MANIT’s Rolta Incubation Centre supports robotics startups, nurturing innovation and entrepreneurship in hardware and IoT.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend the workshop?

Students from any year or department at MANIT or other colleges, with basic programming knowledge.

Do I need my own equipment?

No, all tools (Arduino kits, sensors, laptops) are provided during the workshop.

Will I get a certificate?

Certificates are awarded to top performers in the project competition.

How does this prepare me for competitions?

The workshop covers skills used in events like E-Yantra and national automotive competitions, giving you a head start.
